ABOUT ME

I'm a Spanish girl and I'm a teacher, but I've always liked technology. Now I'm trying to learn to code to change my world. I would like to develop websites and use my coding skills to get a better job.

"Sharing is good, and with digital technology, sharing is easy"

Spain

I got my first computer when I was only 9 years old, with no internet, of course. I spent all my free time on my new computer, installing new games, playing with the settings... I enjoyed making designs of different colors and shapes, always behind the computer.But when I finished high school I decided to become a teacher, I finished my degree and studied two masters, Physical Education and Health and Physical Activity. Later, I spent two more years studying to become an official teacher in my country. But I never felt like that is what I wanted to do, that is why I decided to move to another country and learn more languages.

Belgium

I found myself in Belgium, where I spent two amazing years, working as an Aupair, teaching Spanish and traveling like crazy. After that, I decided I need a change again and return to what my passion was when I was a little girl - The Computer.

The Netherlands

More than a year ago I arrived in windy and rainy Amsterdam, The Netherlands. I got a part-time job that allowed me to study during the day, so I continued learning more about the web, the internet and web creation, a little bit of design, HTML, CSS, Javascript.

I joined Codaisseur, where I learned about React, Redux, Node.js. I built Rest API's using ExpressJs, Docker, Postgres, and Sequelize. I quickly joined "Eat My Ride", a mobile software company that provides cyclists with smart food and nutrition planning solutions, where I was responsible for implementing the whole redesigned front-end of the iOS app's development.
